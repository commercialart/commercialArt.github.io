Positive Message Poster

Be Yourself Poster

by Erik Zhang

This was a very meaningful project to me, as I often feel down, and I think the thesis of the project is very important.

I thought that my design was super creative, as well as representative of the message, as the inside of a head is where you are truly yourself. I was also glad that I had the technical know-how in order to even create this project (even though a professional would laugh at the little amount of technicality necessary.

I liked that I also extended the color background to the text, and this piece has been hung up on campus!
