Final Art Show

Phone Cases made for the Final Art Show

by Erik Zhang

I enjoyed this project because it was very open; I could do practically any sort of art form I wanted to.

As such, I created a project for one of my favorite YouTube channels, which focuses on growth through "seeking discomfort". Because their videos are ambitious, it means that they only upload once a week, which doesn't generate that much revenue for a group of several people. As such, they've recently looked to make money from merchandise.

Using similar fonts and vibes familiar to their videos, I created marketable phone cases that could be sold. I think that these fundamentally work because of the emphasis on the letters and the message, which is what supporters of the channel would likely want emphasized. The solitary feeling of all of the background photos also points out that the best moments of discomfort come alone.

The lines and contrast likewise also are important for the design. All of these images are from places I visited, which means that the cases also resonate with me personally.
